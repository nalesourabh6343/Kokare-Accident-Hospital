First Aid Instructions

Emergency first aid guidance for fractures, dislocations, and bone injuries. Know what to do before the ambulance arrives.

Fracture Care Immobilization RICE Method
View First Aid Guide
First Aid for Bone Injuries
For Suspected Fractures
  • Do NOT move the injured limb
  • Apply ice wrapped in cloth (not directly)
  • Immobilize with splint if available
  • Keep the person calm and still
RICE Method
  • Rest - Stop using the injured area
  • Ice - Apply cold pack for 20 min
  • Compression - Wrap with bandage
  • Elevation - Raise above heart level
Important: Call emergency services immediately for severe injuries, open fractures, or if the person is unconscious.

Our Specialties

Orthopedic Surgeries & Treatments

Advanced surgical techniques and comprehensive treatment for all bone and joint conditions.

Joint Replacement (Knee & Hip)

Advanced total and partial knee/hip replacement surgeries using minimally invasive techniques. Faster recovery with modern implants and computer-assisted navigation.

Spine & Back Surgery

Comprehensive spine care including disc herniation, spinal stenosis, scoliosis correction, and minimally invasive spine surgery (MISS) for faster recovery.

Arthroscopy (Keyhole Surgery)

Minimally invasive joint surgery through tiny incisions. Used for ACL reconstruction, meniscus repair, rotator cuff repair, and cartilage restoration.

Ilizarov Surgery (Russian Method)

Specialized external fixator technique for limb lengthening, deformity correction, bone transport, and complex non-union fracture treatment.

Fracture & Complex Trauma

24/7 emergency trauma care with advanced fracture fixation, polytrauma management, pelvis & acetabulum surgery, and rapid recovery protocols.

Sports Injury & Ligament Surgery

Comprehensive sports medicine for ACL, PCL, MCL injuries, shoulder dislocations, tennis elbow, and performance optimization for athletes.
