# Project Overview: Kokare Accident Hospital Website

This document provides a detailed overview of the structure and sections of the `index.html` file for the Kokare Accident Hospital website.

## Technology Stack
- **HTML5 & CSS3**: Custom styles with CSS variables for branding.
- **Bootstrap 5**: Responsive layout and components (Navbar, Carousel, Accordion, Modals).
- **JavaScript**: Custom logic for scroll progress, navbar behavior, and gallery filtering.
- **Iconography**: Bootstrap Icons.
- **Typography**: Google Fonts (Poppins & Playfair Display).
- **Animations**: AOS (Animate On Scroll) library.

## Visual & Functional Sections (Order of Appearance)

1.  **Scroll Progress Bar**: A thin teal bar at the top that tracks page scroll depth.
2.  **Info Bar**: A teal top strip containing emergency contact numbers and physical location.
3.  **Navigation Bar**:
    - Sticky header with the hospital logo.
    - Mega-dropdowns for About Us, Emergency, Specialties, Facilities, Patient Corner, and Contact Us.
    - "Book Now" call-to-action button.
4.  **Hero Slider**: 
    - Full-width automatic carousel with 3 slides.
    - Focuses on Fracture Care, Joint Replacement, and Spine Care.
5.  **Hospital Stats**: Counter section showing years of experience, patients treated, and surgeries performed.
6.  **Emergency Services**: Highlighted section for 24/7 care, ambulance services, and first aid.
7.  **Golden Hour Protocol**: Four-step guide for immediate accident response.
8.  **About Us**: Detailed introduction to the hospital's history and mission.
9.  **Doctor Profiles**: Dedicated sub-sections for Dr. Yuvraj Kokare and Dr. Dipali Kokare.
10. **OPD Schedule**: 
    - Desktop: Structured table view.
    - Mobile: Card-based layout for better readability on small screens.
11. **Specialized Services**: Grid of orthopedic treatments (Joint replacement, Spine surgery, Arthroscopy, etc.).
12. **Hospital Facilities**: Showcases the modern infrastructure (Operation Theater, X-Ray, Physio, Lab).
13. **Advanced Technology**: Highlights high-tech surgical equipment like C-Arm and Digital DR systems.
14. **Specialist Team**: Additional grid of expert doctors across various orthopedic sub-fields.
15. **Filterable Photo Gallery**: Categorized hospital images (Surgeries, Facilities, Events, Patients).
16. **Appointment Booking**: A comprehensive contact form for patients to schedule visits.
17. **Insurance & Schemes**: 
    - Premium cards for Cashless Insurance (TPA).
    - Details on Government Health Schemes (Ayushman Bharat, MJPJAY).
18. **Health Blog**: Bone care tips and educational articles.
19. **Patient Testimonials**: A carousel of reviews and stories from past patients.
20. **FAQ Section**: Accordion-style list of common orthopedic questions.
21. **Contact & Map**: 
    - Quick contact cards.
    - Google Maps integration for location.
22. **Footer**: Navigation links, social media integration, and hospital branding.
23. **Floating Action Buttons (FAB)**: Persistent icons for WhatsApp chat, direct phone call, and "Back to Top" navigation.
